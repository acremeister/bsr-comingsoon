The Property

Wooded, well-prepared acreage in the heart of East Texas

Welcome to Big Sandy Ranchettes — a carefully curated offering of private, wooded acreage tracts in Polk County, Texas. Each tract is 9.5 acres or more of mature East Texas timber, giving you the canopy, the privacy, and the quiet this part of the state is known for.

We've done the preparation work so you can focus on your vision. Trails have been thoughtfully cut and selective forestry mulching completed to showcase the natural beauty of the land without stripping what makes it special. Power is already extended to the property, and fiber optic internet runs to every lot — a rare infrastructure advantage in a rural setting this private.

Situated just across from our sister development Trails at Benny Griffin, Big Sandy Ranchettes shares the same gravel county road access, sandy loam soils, and peaceful Polk County setting — minutes from the Alabama-Coushatta Indian Reservation in southeastern Polk County.

Location

Polk County peace with practical East Texas reach

Big Sandy Ranchettes sits in the southeastern corridor of Polk County — an area defined by deep piney woods, quiet county roads, and genuine rural character. Located near Benny Griffin Road in the Dallardsville area, the property puts you close to the Alabama-Coushatta Indian Reservation and within a short drive of Livingston amenities.

Private enough to feel truly remote. Practical enough to be your full-time home, weekend haven, or family legacy land.

  • Livingston area services & dining — approx. 25–30 minutes
  • Alabama-Coushatta Indian Reservation — minutes away
  • Big Thicket National Preserve — nearby recreational access
  • Lake Livingston State Park — scenic outdoor recreation close by
  • Houston metro — approx. 1.5 hours via US-190

FAQs

Common questions from acreage buyers

Are tracts available now?

Yes — several tracts are currently available. Inventory moves as reservations and closings occur. Contact us for a real-time status update and current pricing on each available parcel.

How large are the tracts?

Each tract at Big Sandy Ranchettes is 9.5 acres or larger. Exact acreage varies slightly by tract. Contact us for current survey data on each available parcel.

Is power available on the property?

Yes. Sam Houston Electric Cooperative service has been extended to the property, eliminating one of the largest upfront infrastructure costs for rural land buyers.

Is there internet service?

Yes — fiber optic internet is available on every lot. This is a significant and rare advantage for buyers who plan to work remotely, stream, or manage farm and ranch systems from the property.

Is any of the land in a floodplain?

No. Big Sandy Ranchettes has no floodplain concerns. We recommend buyers independently verify this with their lender and surveyor for their selected tract before purchase.

Will I need a well and septic system?

Yes. Municipal water and sewer are not available in this rural area. Plan for a private well and a septic system. We can point you toward local providers for guidance and cost estimates.

Can my realtor represent me?

Absolutely. Licensed buyer agents are welcome and encouraged to register early in the process. Contact Walker Powell directly at (936) 661-9442 to discuss co-broker arrangements.

Can I schedule a showing?

Yes. Showings are by appointment so we can provide current access guidance and tract-specific context. Use the contact form below or call (936) 661-9442 to schedule your visit.

What can I use the land for?

Tracts are suitable for residential use, manufactured homes, homesteading, hunting, recreation, investment, and family land holding. Confirm deed restrictions and local regulations for your specific intended use before purchase.

Is financing available?

Land financing may be available through third-party lenders. Buyers should contact their preferred lender for current land loan terms, down payment requirements, and qualification criteria.
